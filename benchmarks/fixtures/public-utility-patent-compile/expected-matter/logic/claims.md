# Claims

### CLM01 - Flying-machine control combination

A flying-machine comprising:
- superposed connected aeroplanes;
- means for moving opposite lateral portions of the aeroplanes to different angles; and
- a vertical rudder moved toward a side presenting a smaller angle of incidence.
