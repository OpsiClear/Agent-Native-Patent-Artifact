### PA01 - Plain spring-return hinge

A self-closing hinge in which a helical torsion spring housed in the hinge barrel returns the door
toward the closed position. The spring tension is field-adjustable. No damping element is present, so
the door closes at a speed governed only by the spring and the door's inertia.

### PA02 - Surface-mounted overhead door closer

A separate, surface-mounted overhead door closer with a hydraulic cylinder and an adjustable valve that
controls the closing speed of a door. The closer mounts to the door face and the frame header; it is
not integrated into a hinge and provides no spring hinge leaves and no in-hinge hold-open detent (a
hold-open feature, if present, is provided by a separate arm friction brake).
