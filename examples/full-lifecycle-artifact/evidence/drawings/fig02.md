### FIG02 - Method flowchart

A flowchart of the power-free door-closing method: storing energy in the closing spring by opening the
door, releasing the door so the spring drives it closed, displacing working fluid through the hydraulic
damper to limit the closing speed, and optionally holding the door at the hold-open angle until the
release torque is exceeded.
