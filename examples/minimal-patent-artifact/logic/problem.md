# Problem

## Field of invention
Self-watering plant containers; passive (power-free) irrigation level control.

## Problem (O01)
Houseplant reservoirs are easy to overfill, which drowns roots and overflows onto surfaces. Existing
wicking pots meter water *out* of a reservoir but do not regulate the water level put *into* it.

## The gap prior art leaves (G01)
Wicking reservoirs (see PA01) draw water to soil but rely on the user to judge fill level. No passive,
power-free element in the cited art **closes a refill path when the reservoir reaches a target level**.
