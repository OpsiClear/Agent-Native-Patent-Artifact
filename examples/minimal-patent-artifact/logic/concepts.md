# Defined claim terms (lexicography)

> The antecedent-basis and definiteness source of truth. A term of degree must carry an objective bound
> (35 USC 112(b)).

### TERM01 - selected level

A predetermined fill height of the reservoir at which the valve fully closes. The "selected level" is
fixed by the float geometry and the valve seat position; in the disclosed embodiment it is the height
at which the float's upper surface reaches the valve seat (see SPEC0004). It is an objective,
structurally-determined height, not a subjective judgment.
