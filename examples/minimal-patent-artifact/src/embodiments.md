### SPEC0004 - Valve (14) and the selected level

[0004] A valve 14 is mechanically coupled to the float 12 and is positioned at a refill inlet of the
reservoir 10. As the water level rises, the float 12 lifts the valve 14 toward a valve seat; when the
float 12 reaches a selected level - the height at which the float's upper surface meets the valve seat
- the valve 14 fully closes the refill inlet, halting further filling. The selected level is fixed by
the geometry of the float 12 and the position of the valve seat.
